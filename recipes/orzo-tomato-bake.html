Orzo tomato bake

Description

In this recipe you cook the orzo directly in the oven tray with the other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 plum tomatoes
  • a cup of orzo pasta
  • 500ml stock
  • red onions

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees
  2. Put the red onions in the tray and bake for 15 mins or until soft
  3. Drain the tomatoes (saving the juice) and add them to the tray. Bake for 15 mins
  4. Add the pasta with the stock and tomato juice. Bake for 20 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven. People can add halloumi to their part if they eat cheese.
