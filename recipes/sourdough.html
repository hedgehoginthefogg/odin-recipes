Sourdough

Description

This is a great recipe for no-knead sourdough. It takes a couple of days start to finish so plan ahead, but the hands-on labour time is low.

Ingredients

  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 400g flour (I use 200g white, 200g brown)
  • 300ml water
  • 10g salt

Steps

  1. Put the starter, flour water and salt in a bowl. Mix well with a spoon. Leave for 30 mins.
  2. Fold the mixutre with your hands. Leave for 30 mins.
  3. Fold the mixture again with your hands. Leave for 30 mins.
  4. Final time! Fold again and leave uncovered in the bowl for 8 hours.
  5. Flour a chopping board and put the dough on it. Fold a few times, then leave for 30 mins.
  6. While you're waiting, clean the bowl and leave to dry.
  7. Fold again. Line the bowl with baking paper and put the mixture in the bowl.
  8. Put the mixture in the fridge, covered, for 24 hours.
  9. Preheat the oven to around 230 degrees.
  10. Take the bread mixture out of the fridge and score the top with a bread knife. Then put the mixture in a dutch oven, still with the baking paper on. Bake for 35 mins.
  11. Take the lid off the dutch oven. Bake for a further 10 mins.
  12. Take the bread out and leave for at least 30 mins before eating.
